Method used:

1

Reference

Import the original clip and set it as the visual guide for timing, poses, and motion arcs.

2

RotoBrush Each Frame

Use Roto Brush to trace the subject, then step through frame-by-frame to correct edges and refine the matte.

3

Isolate & Fill

Separate the character from the background, then paint/fill shapes and colors to create the final stylized frames.
